how to prevent

how to prevent virus

wash your place

Clean the frequently contacted surfaces

maintain distance

Maintain 6ft distance from other people

don't touch face

Never touch your eyes, mouth or nose without clean hands

wash your hand

Wash your hands frequently and regularly

Cover your mouth

Cover your mouth using a napkin or your elbow while sneezing or coughing.

wear a mask

Always wear a mask when going out or when you are in crowded places

things you should DO

wash your hand

Frequently wash your hands with soap or cleaan it with sanitizer

always wear a mask

Wear masks while going out or while you are in crowded places

stay home during fever

When you are feeling unwell avoid contact with people and stay at home

things you should NOT DO

avoid close contact with animals

Animals directly don't spread covid-19 but their fur can contain particles from the infected

avoid close contact with people

Even though masks and vaccine curb the spread of covid-19, prevent getting in close contact with people

avoid crowded area

Crowded places have high rate of spreading the infection, so avoid unnecessary gatherings
